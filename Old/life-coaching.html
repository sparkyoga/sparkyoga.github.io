Life Coaching

Do you want more connection with friends and family?

Do you want to love your current career more or shift gears entirely?

Do you have big dreams but no real idea of how to reach them?

As a Yoga Life Coach™, Pauline can help you shift into the life that you really want to live - it’s quicker and easier than you think! It’s not therapy or consulting. You won’t be given advice or told what to do. There is no “wishing for” or “manifesting” theories as sole strategies towards what you want. You have to be willing to take action, declare what you want to the people around you, and be honest with your coach in order to move forward.

Getting Started

1. Schedule your first complementary 30 min session via phone
2. After the initial session, both parties will decide if there is a good fit
3. You decide if you want to move forward and schedule your next session
4. You and Pauline will co-create forward momentum and design activities/homework that you will complete before your next session. Pauline will incorporate yoga, meditation and wellness into the coaching, as appropriate.
